Perched on a cliff with views of Khandala Ghat, Dukes Retreat is a 20-minute drive from Lonavala. Surrounded by waterfalls and forest, it has free Wi-Fi and an outdoor pool. Dukes Retreat is 5 km from Lonavala Railway Station. The nearest airport is in Pune, about 60 km from the hotel. Fitted with a fan, rooms at Dukes Retreat feature floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the mountains and beyond. Each is equipped with a flat-screen cable/satellite TV and minibar. A comfortable sofa seating area is also included. For a refreshing workout, guests can visit the fitness centre or play a game of tennis on the on-site courts. The hotel also provides a helpful tour desk and business centre. Panaroma Café serves a variety of international dishes and local cuisine. Cocktails and liquor can be enjoyed at High Point Bar.
